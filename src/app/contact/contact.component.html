Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new match?

Tap the + button on the Matches tab. Select your players, enter the score, and save. The match is recorded instantly.

Can I delete a match or player?

Yes. Swipe left on any match or player in the list to reveal the delete option. Deleted data is removed from your device permanently.

Does CourtClash require an internet connection?

No. CourtClash works entirely offline. All your match data and player stats are stored locally on your device — no account or connection required.

How do I back up my data?

Your data is automatically included in your iCloud or iTunes device backup if you have backups enabled in iOS Settings. Restoring from a backup will restore your CourtClash data.
