Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

CourtClash is designed with privacy at its core. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the CourtClash mobile application.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

CourtClash does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Advertising identifiers or tracking data
  • Any other personal or non-personal information

3. Local Data Storage

All data you create in CourtClash—including match records, player names, and statistics—is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server or third party. You retain full control over your data and can delete it at any time by removing the app.

4. Third-Party Services

CourtClash does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information is protected by your device's security features and your iOS device passcode or biometric authentication.

6. Children's Privacy

CourtClash does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
